# Statement
Let $f_{1},\dots,f_{m}$ be $m$ solutions of the homogeneous linear differential equation
$$a_{0}(x)y^{(n)} + a_{1}(x)y^{(n-1)} + \dots + a_{n-1}(x)y' + a_{n}(x)y = 0.$$
Then $c_{1}f_{1}+\dots+c_{m}f_{m}$ is also a solution of the equation.
