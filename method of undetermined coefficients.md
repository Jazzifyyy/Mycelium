$$a_{0}y^{(n)} + a_{1}y^{(n-1)}+\dots+a_{n}y = F(x)$$
where $F$ is a finite linear combination 
$$F = \sum_{i=1}^m A_{i}u_{m}$$
of UC functions $u_{1},\dots,u_{m}$ and $A_{i}$ being known constants.
