# Notation
The first-order differential equations may be expressed in either the *derivative form*:
$$\frac{dy}{dx} = f(x,y)$$
or the differential form
$$M(x,y)dx + N(x,y)dy = 0.$$
An equation in one of these forms may readily be written in the other form.
