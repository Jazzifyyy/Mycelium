# Definition
The normal form of a linear system of $n$ differential equations in $n$ unknown functions $x_{1},\dots,x_{n}$ is
$$\begin{align}
x_{1}'&= a_{11}(t)x_{1}+\dots+a_{1n}(t)x_{n} + F_{1}(t) \\
&\vdots \\
x_{n}' &= a_{n1}(t)x_{1} + \dots + a_{nn}(t)x_{n}  + F_{n}(t).
\end{align}$$
