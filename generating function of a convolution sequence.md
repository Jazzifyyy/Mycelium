# Statement
Suppose $A(s)$ and $B(s)$ are the generating functions of $\{ a_{n} \}$ and $\{ b_{n} \}$ respectively.

Then the generating function of the [[convolution of two sequences|convolution sequence]] $\{ c_{n} \}$ is given by $A(s)B(s)$.
