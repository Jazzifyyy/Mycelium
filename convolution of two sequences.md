# Definition
Suppose $\{ a_{n} \}$ and $\{ b_{n} \}$ are two sequences. Then the convolution of $\{ a_{n} \}$ and $\{ b_{n} \}$ is a new sequence $\{ c_{n} \}$ defined as
$$c_{n} = \sum_{k=0}^n a_{k}b_{n-k}.$$
