# Statement
Suppose the roots $\lambda_{1}$ and $\lambda_{2}$ of the [[characteristic equation of a homogeneous linear system of differential equations with constant coefficients|characteristic equation]] associated with the [[homogeneous linear systems with constant coefficients - two equation in two unknown functions]] system are real and distinct. 

Then the system has two non-trivial *linearly independent* solutions of the form 
$$\begin{align}
x &= A_{1}e ^{\lambda_{1}t} \\
y &= B_{1}e ^{\lambda_{1}t} 
\end{align}$$
and
$$\begin{align}
x &= A_{2}e^{\lambda_{2}t} \\
y &= B_{2}e^{\lambda_{2}t}
\end{align}$$
where $A_{1},B_{1},A_{2},B_{2}$ are determined by solving the characteristic equation. 

The general solution of the system may then be written as
$$\begin{align}
x &= c_{1}A_{1}
\end{align}$$
