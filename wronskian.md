# Definition
Suppose $f_{1},\dots,f_{n}$ are $n$ real functions each of which has an $(n-1)$ derivative on $[a,b]$. The determinant
$$W(f_{1},\dots,f_{n}) = \begin{vmatrix}
f_{1} & \dots & f_{n} \\
f_{1}' & \dots & f_{n}'
\end{vmatrix}$$
